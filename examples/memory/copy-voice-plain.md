Every piece of Driftwatch copy — landing page, emails, in-app text, release
notes — is plain, short, and specific. Banned: "supercharge", "seamless",
"game-changing", "unlock", exclamation points, and any sentence that could
appear on a competitor's site unchanged. Stated 2026-05-22 after Maya
rewrote a drafted landing hero from scratch; repeated 2026-06-18.

**Why:** Driftwatch sells to skeptical data-literate buyers; hype reads as
a signal the product is thin.
**How to apply:** draft copy at half the length that feels natural, name
the concrete mechanism ("flags accounts 21 days before renewal") instead
of the benefit adjective, and never add an exclamation point.
